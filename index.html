About She Can Foundation

She Can Foundation is a non-governmental organisation registered under the Indian Societies Act, 1860. We are dedicated to empowering women and creating a more equitable society for all.

We provide support, resources, and training to women in communities across the globe, working closely with local organisations, governments, and communities to ensure our programs are effective and sustainable.

We rely on the support of individuals, corporations, and partner organisations to achieve our vision. Together, we can revolutionise society and create a better world for all.

What We Do

Four ways we create lasting change for women.

🎓 Training & Education

We provide skills training and educational resources to help women build confidence and independence.

📢 Advocacy

Through campaigns and initiatives, we raise awareness of women's issues and push for lasting policy change.

🤝 Community Support

We work hand in hand with local organisations and governments to deliver programs that are grounded in community needs.

🌍 Global Outreach

Our reach extends across the globe, partnering with corporations and individuals who share our vision of equity.
